Frequently asked

About Shrtn

Are links persistent?

Yes. Link records are stored in PostgreSQL and remain available across restarts.

Does Shrtn track clicks?

Yes. Each redirect increments the click count and updates the last-clicked timestamp.

Does Shrtn create QR codes?

Yes. The browser extension generates downloadable QR codes for short links.

Is there a public API?

Yes. Shrtn includes documented endpoints for creation, redirects, analytics, and health.
